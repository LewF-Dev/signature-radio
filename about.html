THE JOURNEY

Signature Radio UK was built on authentic vision, a passion and a number of local DJs and presenters with a genuine love for music.

Online, we quickly gained a dedicated community of 80,000 listeners who valued a proactive and responsive station.

From these organic roots, we have evolved into a premier broadcasting powerhouse. On May 1st, 2026, we officially launch on DAB, expanding 'The Soundtrack Of Your Life' across Bristol, South Gloucestershire, North Somerset & Severnside to a potential audience of 1.2 million listeners — available in car, smart devices and online.

Our doctrine remains sharp: Anticipate, Mitigate, then Legislate.
